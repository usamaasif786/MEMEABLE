Frequently Asked Question

What is Memeable?

Memeable is a revolutionary meme-inspired cryptocurrency that aims to create a vibrant community around internet humor and expression. It leverages blockchain technology to immortalize iconic internet memes as unique and collectible digital assets called Meme NFTs.

How can I participate in Memeable?

You can participate in Memeable by purchasing MEME tokens, which serve as the native currency of the Memeable ecosystem. Visit our website or supported exchanges to acquire MEME tokens and join the MemeVerse.

What are Meme NFTs?

Meme NFTs are non-fungible tokens that represent specific iconic internet memes. Each Meme NFT is unique and can be owned, traded, and cherished within the Memeable ecosystem. They celebrate the essence of internet culture and allow meme creators to be part of the revolution.

How are Meme NFTs created?

Meme creators can mint their original meme characters and moments as Meme NFTs on the Memeable platform. These NFTs can then be sold, auctioned, or kept as collectible tokens within the MemeVerse.

What makes Memeable different from other meme coins?

Memeable goes beyond the typical hype of meme coins. It embraces the culture of memes by creating a decentralized and interactive ecosystem. The Meme NFTs allow meme creators to leave a lasting mark on the internet and engage with the community in a fun and rewarding manner.

How are taxes implemented in Memeable?

Memeable implements a 0.5% buy and sell tax. When users buy or sell MEME tokens, 0.5% of the transaction amount is burned, reducing the token supply. This burning mechanism adds scarcity to the token and supports long-term value growth.

How is the token supply distributed?

Upon deployment, 80% of the total token supply goes to the liquidity wallet, 10% to the CEX listing wallet, 5% to the donation wallet, and the remaining 5% to the community rewards wallet. This distribution ensures a strong foundation for the Memeable ecosystem.

How can I contribute to the Memeable community?

As a member of the Memeable community, you can actively participate by creating and sharing original memes, engaging in meme battles, voting on new features, and supporting charitable initiatives. Your creativity and involvement drive the growth of the MemeVerse.

Is Memeable safe to use?

Yes, Memeable is built on blockchain technology, ensuring transparency, security, and immutability of transactions. However, always exercise caution while engaging in any cryptocurrency-related activities, and be wary of potential scams or fraudulent schemes.

Where can I learn more about Memeable and join the community?

For more information and to join the vibrant Memeable community, visit our official website and social media channels. Stay updated with the latest news, events, and opportunities within the MemeVerse.
